What is BTS SIO?

The Brevet de Technicien Supérieur in Information Technology Services to Organizations (BTS SIO) is aimed at those who wish to train in two years for careers as network administrators or developers. This prepares them to directly enter the job market or continue their studies in the field of computer science.


The BTS SIO offers two specialties:


Option SISR

The Infrastructure Solutions, Systems, and Networks option trains professionals in computer networks and equipment (installation, maintenance, security). Graduates of the BTS SIO SISR are capable of managing and administering a company's network and ensuring its security and maintenance.
Technicians with a higher diploma in computer science specializing in SISR can access jobs such as:

    Network and Systems Administrator
    IT Support Technician
    Operations Controller
    Systems and Network Support
    Infrastructure Technician
    Production Technician
    Micro and Network Technician

Option SLAM

The Software Solutions and Business Applications option trains specialists in software (drafting specifications, formulating needs and specifications, development, integration within the company).
Technicians with a higher diploma in computer science specializing in SLAM are prepared for jobs such as:

    Computer Application Developer
    Software Developer
    Application or Study Analyst
    Programmer Analyst
    Application Programmer
    Manager of Application Services
    Computer Studies Technician

Technology Watch

What is technology watch?

Technology watch, an integral component of strategic intelligence, entails the systematic monitoring of technical advancements and innovations within a specific industry sector. It encompasses surveillance, data collection, sharing, and dissemination, enabling anticipation and informed decision-making concerning changes in research, development, patents, product launches, materials, processes, manufacturing innovations, and more. The ultimate objective is to assess their impact on both the environment and organizational strategies.


Key tools for my watch

Utilizing newsletters such as AppyGeek and Google Alert, alongside preferred websites like Zataz, which specializes in cybercrime reporting, complements my regular information intake. These resources furnish me with the latest updates, enabling me to stay abreast of technological advancements.


Subject of my watch

For my technological surveillance, I have chosen to focus on the following: The impact of artificial intelligence in enterprises.


Artificial intelligence (AI) has become indispensable across numerous industries. Its integration within enterprises revolutionizes processes, business models, and customer interactions. This surveillance topic aims to explore AI's diverse applications in enterprises, including natural language processing, computer vision, recommendation systems, among others. Furthermore, it necessitates a comprehensive analysis of the benefits, challenges, and ethical implications arising from the escalating utilization of AI in professional settings.
